Expense Tracker

Project Intro

Expense Tracker is a smart web app for managing daily income and expenses easily. Developed by Premier University CSE Dept (Batch 46) as an academic IP lab final project.

Problem Statement

Students and professionals often struggle to manage their expenses due to different financial needs and lack of a simple, organized tracking system.

Our Solution

Expense Tracker provides two customized modes (Student and General) with an easy-to-use interface to help users record, monitor, and manage their expenses effectively.
